CS Club

The CS Club is both a room and a group of computer science majors and associates contained therein. Back in the days of yore, most members of the Game Dev Club were CS majors, because games necessarily involve programming, so there was a lot of overlap.

In honor of this tradition, and because it's convenient, we still use the Computer Science Clubroom for certain events.

The CS Club is in MacQuarrie Hall, room 227, on the second floor. It's open whenever there's an officer present; usually this is from about 9am to 11pm. MacQuarrie Hall, on the other hand, closes at 10pm, so if you want to get in past then, you'll have to get someone in the Club to let you into the building.

There's no good way to do this. Most of us resort to throwing rocks at the window.
